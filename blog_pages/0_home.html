Bio

Hello! I am Sedona (she/her), a dual B.S./B.A student at Columbia University. My Bachelor of Science was in computer science with a focus on the variety of applications of computer technologies, and I am currently finishing up my Bachelor of Arts in human rights with a senior thesis on discrmination in predictive policing software. I am extremely interested in the intersection of these fields, specifically regarding how we can mitigate the effects of algorithmic discrimination.

Adovcacy Campaign

This advocacy campaign will focus on the effects of mass data collection across domains of computer science, including artificial intelligence, natural language processing, and computer vision. As the use of artificial intelligence has become more widespread, there are significant misconceptions about the capabilities and limitations of these technologies. With my background in computer science and experience with software development, I am in the unique position to help contexualize misconceptions about these technologies and highlight the significant limitations in the regulations of digital systems.
