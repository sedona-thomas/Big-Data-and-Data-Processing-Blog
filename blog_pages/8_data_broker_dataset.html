Data Brokers Dataset

Privacy Rights Clearinghouse is a nonprofit with the intention of increasing access to data privacy information and resources. They have compiled a comprehevsive dataset of data brokers that can be found at privacyrights.org. There are currently 232 data brokers listed, but there are significantly more brokers actively aggragating and selling personal data. In California and Vermont alone, since enacting laws requiring data brokers to register with a state registry, there were 540 unique data brokers registered by July 2021. Recent legislation has also been introduced in other states to require data brokers to register with a state registry, including New Jersey.

Try It Yourself

Online data brokers like Spokeo, BeenVerified, TruthFinder, and US Search. Personal tests of each of these sites revealed that Spokeo and TruthFinder have the most consistently obvious results that can clearly identify a unique person at the free tier with only a name. Try it out yourself by opening the site and following the instructions to submit your name and see if you can be uniquely identified.
